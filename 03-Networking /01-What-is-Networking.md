> "Networking is simply the process of connecting devices so they can communicate and share information."

# 🤔 What is Networking?

Networking is the process of connecting two or more devices so they can communicate and share data.

These devices can include:

- Computers
- Laptops
- Mobile Phones
- Printers
- Servers
- Routers
- Smart TVs
- CCTV Cameras
- IoT Devices

Once connected, these devices can exchange information.

---

# 🌍 Real-Life Example

Imagine you and your friend are in different rooms.

If you want to talk to your friend, you use your phone.

Your voice travels through the mobile network.

Similarly, computers use a network to send and receive data.

---

# 💻 Everyday Examples of Networking

You use networking every day without realizing it.

Examples include:

- Browsing websites
- Watching YouTube
- Sending emails
- Playing online games
- Using WhatsApp
- Video calls
- Downloading files
- Online shopping
- Cloud storage
- Banking apps

All of these depend on networking.

---

# ❓ Why Do We Need Networking?

Networking allows devices to:

- Share files
- Access the Internet
- Share printers
- Communicate quickly
- Access cloud services
- Work together efficiently

Without networking, every computer would work independently.

---

# 🌐 What is a Network?

A network is simply a group of connected devices that communicate with each other.

Example:

```
Laptop
     │
     │
Router
 ┌───┼───┐
 │   │   │
PC Phone Printer
```

All devices are connected through a router and can communicate with one another.

---

# 🛡 Why is Networking Important in Cybersecurity?

Every cyber attack travels through a network.

For example:

- Phishing emails
- Malware
- Ransomware
- Website attacks
- Password attacks
- Data theft

If you understand networking, you can better understand how these attacks work and how to defend against them.
