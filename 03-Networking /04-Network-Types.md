# 📖 4. Types of Networks

> "Not every network is the same. Different networks are designed for different purposes."

---

# 🎯 Learning Objectives

After completing this topic, you will:

- Understand the different types of networks.
- Learn where each network is used.
- Identify real-world examples of each network type.

---

# 🤔 Why Are There Different Types of Networks?

Different places have different networking needs.

For example:

- Your phone connects to your smartwatch.
- Your laptop connects to your home Wi-Fi.
- A university connects hundreds of computers.
- The Internet connects billions of devices worldwide.

That's why different types of networks exist.

---

# 🌐 1. PAN (Personal Area Network)

### 📌 What is PAN?

A **Personal Area Network (PAN)** is a small network used to connect personal devices within a short distance (usually a few meters).

### 📱 Examples

- Mobile ↔ Smartwatch
- Mobile ↔ Bluetooth Earbuds
- Laptop ↔ Bluetooth Mouse
- Laptop ↔ Wireless Keyboard

### 📏 Range

Around **10 meters**.

### 🌍 Real-Life Example

Connecting your phone to wireless earbuds using Bluetooth.

---

# 🏠 2. LAN (Local Area Network)

### 📌 What is LAN?

A **Local Area Network (LAN)** connects devices within a small area.

### 📍 Used In

- Homes
- Schools
- Offices
- Computer Labs

### 🌍 Example

Home Wi-Fi connecting:

- Laptop
- Mobile
- Smart TV
- Printer

```
Laptop
   │
Phone ── Router ── Printer
   │
 Smart TV
```

---

# 📶 3. WLAN (Wireless Local Area Network)

### 📌 What is WLAN?

A **Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN)** is a LAN that uses **Wi-Fi instead of cables**.

### 🌍 Example

Your home's Wi-Fi network.

Instead of Ethernet cables, devices connect wirelessly.

---

# 🏫 4. CAN (Campus Area Network)

### 📌 What is CAN?

A **Campus Area Network (CAN)** connects multiple buildings within a campus.

### 📍 Used In

- Colleges
- Universities
- Hospitals
- Large Company Campuses

### 🌍 Example

One university connecting:

- Library
- Hostels
- Administration Block
- Labs

---

# 🏙 5. MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)

### 📌 What is MAN?

A **Metropolitan Area Network (MAN)** connects multiple LANs across a city.

### 📍 Used In

- Government offices
- Banks
- Cable TV providers
- ISPs

### 🌍 Example

A bank connecting all its branches within one city.

---

# 🌍 6. WAN (Wide Area Network)

### 📌 What is WAN?

A **Wide Area Network (WAN)** connects networks across countries or even the entire world.

### 🌍 Best Example

**The Internet**

```
India
   │
USA
   │
Japan
   │
Australia
```

All connected through the Internet.

---

# 💾 7. SAN (Storage Area Network)

### 📌 What is SAN?

A **Storage Area Network (SAN)** is a dedicated network that connects servers to storage devices.

### 📍 Used In

- Data Centers
- Cloud Providers
- Large Companies

### 🌍 Example

A company storing employee and customer data in centralized storage.

---

# 🔒 8. VPN (Virtual Private Network)

### 📌 What is VPN?

A **Virtual Private Network (VPN)** creates a secure and encrypted connection over the Internet.

### 📍 Used For

- Remote work
- Secure browsing
- Protecting data
- Accessing office networks safely

### 🌍 Example

An employee working from home securely connects to the company's network using a VPN.

---

# 📊 Network Types Comparison

| Type | Full Form | Coverage Area | Example |
|------|-----------|---------------|---------|
| PAN | Personal Area Network | Few meters | Bluetooth |
| LAN | Local Area Network | Home/Office | Home Wi-Fi |
| WLAN | Wireless LAN | Home/Office | Wi-Fi |
| CAN | Campus Area Network | Campus | University |
| MAN | Metropolitan Area Network | City | Bank Branches |
| WAN | Wide Area Network | Country/World | Internet |
| SAN | Storage Area Network | Data Center | Server Storage |
| VPN | Virtual Private Network | Anywhere | Remote Office Access |

---

# 💡 Interview Questions

### Q1. What is the difference between LAN and WAN?

A LAN covers a small area like a home or office, while a WAN connects networks across large geographical areas, such as countries.

---

### Q2. Which network type does Bluetooth use?

PAN (Personal Area Network).

---

### Q3. What is the best example of a WAN?

The Internet.

---

### Q4. What is the difference between LAN and WLAN?

LAN can use wired or wireless connections, while WLAN specifically refers to a wireless LAN using Wi-Fi.

---

### Q5. Why is a VPN used?

To create a secure and encrypted connection over the Internet.
