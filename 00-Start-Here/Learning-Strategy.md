# 📖 Learning Strategy

This roadmap follows one simple learning cycle.

Learn

↓

Understand

↓

Take Notes

↓

Practice

↓

Build Projects

↓

Teach Someone Else

↓

Repeat

If you skip any step, your learning will be incomplete.

---

## The 70-20-10 Rule

70% Practical

20% Reading

10% Watching Videos

Don't spend all day watching YouTube.

Spend most of your time doing.

---

## Learn in Public

Create

- GitHub
- LinkedIn
- Personal Portfolio

Upload

- Notes
- Projects
- Lab Writeups
- Cheat Sheets

This becomes your portfolio.
